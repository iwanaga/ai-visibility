Frequently Asked Questions

What’s the best way to get from the airport to Paris?

The RER B train costs €13 and takes 30-40 minutes to central Paris. Taxis cost €50-70 but are more convenient if you have heavy luggage.

Should I buy a Paris Metro pass?

For stays of 3+ days, buy a Navigo weekly pass (€31.60 unlimited travel). For shorter visits, a carnet of 10 tickets costs €17.35.

Is Paris walkable or do I need public transport?

Central Paris is walkable but major attractions are spread out. The Louvre to Eiffel Tower is 2.5 km (30-minute walk). Combine walking with metro for efficiency.
